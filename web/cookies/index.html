Cookie Notice

Effective: 2026-07-01 · Last updated: 2026-07-01

This Cookie Notice explains how Fortune Master ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our application at fortune-master.pages.dev (the "Service").

For broader information on how we handle your personal data, see our Privacy Policy.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. "Local storage" and "session storage" serve similar purposes and are treated the same way for this Notice. "Similar technologies" includes web beacons, pixels, and SDKs.

2. Cookies we use

Currently, we use strictly necessary cookies only. We do not use advertising or analytics cookies.

3. Why only strictly necessary?

  • The Service is a single-developer project; we don't run ad networks.
  • We currently do not perform analytics on user behaviour. If we add analytics in the future, we will ask for consent via a cookie banner before activating them, and update this Notice.
  • The auth cookie is technically required to deliver the Service you signed up for.

4. Your choices

  • Browser settings: You can block or delete cookies in your browser. Note that blocking the Supabase auth cookies will sign you out.
  • Sign out: Use the in-app "Sign out" button to clear the session cookie immediately.
  • Mobile OS controls: iOS and Android provide "Limit Ad Tracking" / "Opt out of Ads Personalisation" settings. These don't affect us because we don't do ad tracking, but they reset ad identifiers on your device.

5. Do Not Track / Global Privacy Control

We respect the Do Not Track (DNT) and Global Privacy Control (GPC) browser signals. If we add analytics or non-strictly-necessary cookies in the future, we will treat DNT/GPC as an opt-out.

6. Changes to this Notice

Material changes will be announced in-app and via this page. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance.
