Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-07-01 · Last updated: 2026-07-01

This Privacy Policy explains how Fortune Master ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you visit our application at fortune-master.pages.dev (the "Service").

The Service is offered by an individual developer (davard123). For privacy-related enquiries, contact privacy@fortunemaster.app.

Entertainment only: The Service provides divination-style content for entertainment and self-reflection. It is NOT a substitute for medical, legal, financial, psychological, or career advice. AI-generated interpretations may contain errors. Use your own judgment.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account data

  • Email address — when you sign up to save readings or post in the community.
  • Display name — chosen by you, defaults to the email local-part.

1.2 Divination inputs (sensitive)

  • Birth date, birth time, birth city, IANA timezone, and gender. Required for chart systems that depend on them (Bazi, Zi Wei, Western astrology).
  • Free-text question (Tarot / I Ching / Qimen) — entirely optional; you can decline.

We deliberately do not collect precise birth latitude / longitude (unlike most astrology products). City-level is enough for our chart precision.

1.3 Computed chart data

The chart inputs above, the computed four-pillars / tarot spread / etc., and any AI-generated interpretation ("llm_interp") you choose to save.

1.4 Community content

  • Posts and comments you create (visible to other users unless you mark them anonymous).
  • Likes and reactions.

1.5 Subscription / payment data

  • Subscription tier, status, and renewal date (managed by RevenueCat for iOS / Android, or Stripe for Web).
  • We never see or store your full credit-card number. Payment data is handled exclusively by our payment processors.

1.6 Technical / device data

  • IP address, basic browser / device metadata, request timestamps — captured by our hosting and edge providers.
  • Aggregated usage analytics (only if you opt in; not active by default).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Generate divination charts you request.
  • Save your reading history so you can revisit it.
  • Generate AI interpretations when you tap "AI Interpretation" (text is sent to our LLM provider along with your chart; see §5).
  • Operate community features and prevent abuse.
  • Manage subscriptions and prevent refund fraud.
  • Respond to abuse reports and legal requests.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Art. 6)

  • Consent — for AI-interpretation calls that leave our infrastructure.
  • Contract — for paying subscribers, to deliver the subscription.
  • Legitimate interest — for security, abuse prevention, and basic service analytics.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use strictly necessary cookies only — set by our auth provider (Supabase) to keep you signed in. We do not set advertising or analytics cookies at this time. See our Cookie Notice.

6. Cross-Border Data Transfers

Our primary hosting is in the United States (Supabase us-west-1, Cloudflare global network). If you are in the EU / EEA, UK, or China, your data is transferred to and processed in the US. Where we use DeepSeek for AI interpretation, chart text is transferred to China. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses for transfers out of the EU/EEA.

7. Retention

  • Account data: until you delete your account, then 30 days for recovery.
  • Chart results and saved interpretations: until you delete them or your account.
  • Posts/comments: until you delete them. If your account is deleted, posts authored are pseudonymised (kept but unlinked).
  • Subscription records: kept for the legally required accounting period (typically 7 years).
  • Server logs: 90 days, then aggregated or deleted.

8. Your Rights

Where local law applies (GDPR, CCPA, PIPL, LGPD), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data in a portable format (JSON)
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Withdraw consent (for AI-interpretation calls)

To exercise any of these, email privacy@fortunemaster.app. We respond within 30 days.

9. Children

The Service is not intended for users under 16 (under 13 in the US, under 14 in some EU jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has registered, contact us for deletion.

10. Security

We use Supabase Row-Level Security, HTTPS everywhere, hashed passwords (handled by Supabase Auth), and access-controlled Edge Functions. No system is perfectly secure; if you discover a vulnerability, please email security@fortunemaster.app responsibly.

11. Changes to This Policy

Material changes will be announced in-app and via this page. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance.
